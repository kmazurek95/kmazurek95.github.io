Collected, validated, and structured ballot data across federal, state, county, city, and district-level elections. Built ETL workflows for political data from inconsistent sources -- different terminologies, non-standard PDFs, and buried information across county election offices.

Developed an automated validation script (cross-referencing names, districts, dates, and flagging duplicates) that reduced error rates from 8% to under 2% and achieved 100% team adoption within two weeks. The tool became part of standard onboarding.

Created repeatable processes for the hardest data categories -- school board races using archived meeting minutes, ballot measure translations from legalistic to plain language -- and documented them for team use.
