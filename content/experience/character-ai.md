Created economics tutor-student dialogues for AI training. After initial feedback showed technically correct but pedagogically ineffective outputs, researched economics education literature, analyzed real tutoring sessions, and studied common student misconception patterns.

Redesigned dialogue creation process around realistic errors, guided discovery, and scaffolded progression. Dialogue approval rate improved from approximately 60% to over 95%. Dialogues were used as examples in contributor guidelines.
