Evaluate and label AI model outputs at scale for Facebook's AI-powered user experience features. Work centers on applying structured evaluation methodology to LLM outputs, identifying systematic failure patterns, and maintaining quality standards across a 15-person team.

Designed repeatable decision rules for edge cases involving misinformation, AI-generated content, and culturally sensitive material. Used audit data systematically to identify high-impact error patterns and reduce them. Maintained quality scores averaging ~95/100.

This role shaped the measurement methodology thinking behind my published article "What Political Science Can Teach AI Evaluation" -- the same construct validity and inter-rater reliability challenges I studied in my MSc apply directly to AI evaluation pipelines.
