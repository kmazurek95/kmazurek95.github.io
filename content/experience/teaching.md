Taught 26 classes per week in humanities and English at a bilingual school serving Palestinian students in Nablus. Developed curriculum materials for the international baccalaureate program. Two years of Arabic language immersion in a region where the political dynamics I had studied in college classrooms were daily lived experience. International studies trains you to think about how international structures shape lived experience. The West Bank made that principle concrete.
